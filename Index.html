CTI Logistics (Lao) Co., Ltd. is a subsidiary of CTI Logistics Co., Ltd. (Bangkok), providing integrated cross-border and domestic logistics solutions from our Vientiane headquarters.

Strategically located in Vientiane, CTI Lao serves as a critical logistics bridge connecting Thailand, Laos, China, Vietnam, and Cambodia — delivering integrated cross-border and domestic transportation solutions.

Our Vision

To be the region's leading logistics partner of choice, reputable and best known for smarter, flexible supply chain solutions and localized expertise across our network.

Our Mission

We go the extra mile to integrate our services to create cost-effective and seamless supply chains for our customers. We listen to and anticipate unique needs while continuously innovating our total solutions.

Our Approach

Different localities present different logistics complexities. We take pride in building localized knowledge to get your shipments delivered on-time. Complexity simplified — that's our promise.

Our Services
Beyond Boundary for
Logistics & Transportation

Comprehensive one-stop logistics solutions covering air, sea, road, and multimodal freight — international and domestic.

International

Air Freight Forwarding

Charter flights, AOG handling, perishable cargo, and door-to-door air freight for time-critical global shipments.

International

Sea Freight Forwarding

FCL and LCL ocean freight with competitive rates via major shipping lines and global port connections.

Cross-Border

Cross-Border Trucking

International trucking via Thailand–Laos–Vietnam–Cambodia–China corridors with full transit documentation.

Compliance

Customs Brokerage

Full-service import/export customs clearance, transit documentation, and regulatory compliance with Lao Customs.

Integrated

Multimodal Transport

Combined air-sea-road-rail solutions leveraging the Laos-China Railway for optimized cost and transit time.

Storage

Warehousing & Distribution

General and specialized storage with inventory management, packing, and distribution services in Vientiane.

Domestic

Domestic Transportation

Nationwide trucking across Lao PDR — Vientiane, Savannakhet, Luang Prabang, Pakse, and all provinces with reliable fleet coverage.

Domestic

Last-Mile & Door-to-Door

Reliable last-mile delivery and pickup services within Vientiane Capital and provincial areas for B2B and project cargo.

Specialized

Project & Special Cargo

Relocation services, exhibition cargo, factory moves, and oversized/heavy-lift transport with dedicated project management.
